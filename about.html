About Smartomation.ai

Our Story

Smartomation.ai was born from a passion for efficient AI. We focus on automation that saves time and money, using tools like N8n.io and Google AI Studio. Our journey began with a simple belief: AI automation should be accessible, cost-effective, and actually work in production environments.

Every product we build runs on N8n.io, an open-source workflow automation platform trusted by enterprises worldwide. We combine modern AI models with robust automation logic to create intelligent systems that think, act, and deliver measurable results.

Founder

Kumar Sundaresan has worked hands-on across multiple global technology companies and has architected demanding, large-scale automation and AI solutions. Smartomation.ai represents the culmination of years of experience building real-world automation solutions that scale and deliver measurable business value.
