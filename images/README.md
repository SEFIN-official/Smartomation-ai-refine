# Image Directory Structure

## Founder Image
**Location:** `images/kumar-sundaresan.jpg`

Place Kumar Sundaresan's professional photo here. The image should be:
- Square format (recommended: 600x600px or larger)
- Professional headshot
- High quality (JPG or PNG)
- If image is not found, the page will fallback to "KS" initials

## Company Logos
**Location:** `images/logos/`

Place all company logo files in the `images/logos/` folder with the following naming convention:

### Technology, AI & Platforms
- `google.svg` (or `.png`, `.jpg`)
- `linkedin.svg`
- `yahoo.svg`
- `hp.svg`
- `garmin.svg`
- `adp.svg`
- `onsemi.svg`
- `lantronix.svg`

### Telecom, Payments & Infrastructure
- `att.svg`
- `verifone.svg`
- `cricket.svg`

### Media, Advertising & Communications
- `nbcuniversal.svg`
- `comcast.svg`
- `wpp.svg`

### Retail, Consumer & Lifestyle
- `nike.svg`
- `gap.svg`
- `columbia.svg`
- `overstock.svg`

### Healthcare & Enterprise Systems
- `cerner.svg`

### Defense, Government & Consulting
- `us-air-force.svg`
- `pwc.svg`
- `csc.svg`
- `gore.svg`

## Logo Requirements
- **Format:** SVG preferred, PNG/JPG also supported
- **Size:** Logos will auto-scale to fit (max 70px height)
- **Background:** Transparent backgrounds recommended
- **Fallback:** If logo image is missing, company name will display as text

## Where to Get Logos
- Company official websites (press/media sections)
- Simple Icons: https://simpleicons.org/
- Logo downloads from company brand guidelines
- Ensure you have permission to use logos
